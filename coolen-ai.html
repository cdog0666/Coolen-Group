Coolen AI

AI-powered text-back automation system that converts missed calls into booked appointments.

Overview

Coolen AI is designed for service-based businesses like plumbers, electricians, salons, and clinics. When a business misses a call, the system automatically sends a smart SMS response, handles basic conversation, and pushes the customer toward booking a calendar slot.

Features

Missed Call Detection

Instantly triggers AI response when a call is not answered.

AI Text Conversations

Handles natural back-and-forth messaging with customers.

Booking Integration

Automatically sends Calendly or booking links to close leads.

How It Works

1. Missed Call

Customer calls business but no one answers.

2. AI Response

System sends instant personalized SMS reply.

3. Conversion

AI guides customer to book appointment automatically.
